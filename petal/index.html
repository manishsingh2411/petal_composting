The Dilemma

Honoring Tradition in a Modern World

Every day, countless flowers offered in devotion face an unfitting end. In urban apartments and societies, without access to soil, families are forced to dispose of these sacred offerings in the bin. This not only feels disrespectful but also contributes to landfill waste, a cycle of waste that feels at odds with the spirit of the offerings.

A Cycle of Guilt
Throwing away sacred flowers can feel like a small sin, a daily conflict between devotion and practicality.
